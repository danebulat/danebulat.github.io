Welcome to my homepage! I am a software developer from the UK with a keen interest in so-called Web3 technologies and functional programming. In addition to Haskell being my favorite programming language, I also have experience developing with JavaScript (HTML5) technologies, databases, and DevOps tools such as Nix. I also hold an MSc in Games Software Development.

Development Setup
